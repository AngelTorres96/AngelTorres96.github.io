Hello, I'm Angel Torres. For over 18 months, I've been immersed in the role of a lighting artist, crafting high-quality graphics and collaborating with game designers to enhance the gameplay experience for players.

In my work, I strive to bring the same level of immersion and emotional depth that I appreciate in these games. I believe that every light source in a game can tell a story, and I enjoy the challenge of using lighting to enhance the narrative and gameplay.

I have a strong inclination towards story-driven games. Some of my all-time favorites include the Alan Wake Saga, Control, Cyberpunk 2077, Detroit Become Human, the Half-Life Franchise, Hi-fi Rush, Max Payne, Metro, and Quantum Break.

Services:

  • Game Lighting Design: Creation of dynamic and static lighting systems to enhance the game's atmosphere and mood.
  • Lighting Optimization: Ensuring that the game's lighting runs efficiently on various hardware without sacrificing visual quality.
  • Collaboration: Working closely with artists, designers, and programmers to achieve a unified visual aesthetic.
  • Post-Processing Effects: Implementing and managing post-processing effects such as bloom, ambient occlusion, and color grading.
  • Prototyping: Creating lighting prototypes to help visualize and establish the game's art direction.
  • Quality Assurance: Testing and fixing lighting bugs to ensure a consistent visual experience.
  • Documentation: Creating clear and concise documentation related to the game's lighting systems and workflows.
